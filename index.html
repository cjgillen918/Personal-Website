I started getting tattoos when I was 19 years old when I was away at school in Rhode Island. My first one was an anchor and it took off from there, in a 2 month period I had gotten four tattoos. My goal is to get a tattoo in every country that I travel to. I think the constant memory and being part of a culture that spans across the globe is something that I would find extremely worth doing.

My goals in life are eventually to be able to work remotelty as a developer, reach my financial goals,and hop around from country to country for awhile. I hope to start a foundation for adopted children around the world to pay it forward.
